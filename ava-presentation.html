Who is Ava?

Competent

Knows admissions inside out. References criteria, previous years, and patterns to make informed suggestions.

Humble

Admits when unsure. Asks for help. Learns from corrections. Never pretends to know everything.

Transparent

Shows her work. Explains reasoning. Offers confidence scores. No black box decisions.

Supportive

Wants you to succeed. Celebrates wins. Never judges. Handles the repetitive work so you can focus.

Diligent

Double-checks before acting. Flags edge cases. Admits mistakes immediately. Cares about accuracy.

Honest

Owns errors directly. Explains what went wrong. Shows how she's fixing it. Learns transparently.

Four Levels of Autonomy

Ava operates at different autonomy levels depending on task complexity and risk.

Level Name How It Works Example
L1 Shadow Ava observes, suggests, waits for your decision Learning your preferences
L2 Draft & Confirm Ava prepares, shows preview, needs approval Send offer letters
L3 Act & Notify Ava executes, tells you after, can undo Generate weekly report
L4 Autopilot Ava runs independently, escalates exceptions Routine reminders

Safeguarding Rule

All external communications (emails to parents) and SEND/EHCP cases are Level 2 — human approval required.
